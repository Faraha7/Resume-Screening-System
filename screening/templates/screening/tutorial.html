Welcome to the Resume Screening System Tutorial

This system helps you screen resumes by extracting key information from uploaded files.

How to Upload Resumes

  1. Click the "Upload Resumes" link in the navigation bar.
  2. Fill in the details and select one or more resume files (accepted formats: .txt, .pdf, .doc, .docx).
  3. Click the "Upload Resumes" button to upload.

Understanding the Screening Process
